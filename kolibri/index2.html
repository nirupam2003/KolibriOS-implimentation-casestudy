Priority Scheduling

CPU scheduling algorithm that assigns priorities to processes, allowing higher priority tasks to be executed first.

Round Robin

Round robin is a CPU scheduling algorithm where each process is assigned a fixed time quantum, and tasks are executed in a cyclic manner, providing equal opportunity for all processes to execute within their allotted time slice.

shortest Job First

SJF selects the process with the smallest total execution time next, ensuring that the shortest jobs are prioritized and executed first.

Banker's Algorithm

It ensures safe execution of processes by simulating their resource requests and releases to determine if granting those requests will lead to a safe state, preventing deadlocks and ensuring efficient resource utilization.

Kolibri OS is a lightweight and open-source operating system that offers impressive functionality in a compact package. Designed to run efficiently on older hardware or low-power devices, Kolibri OS is known for its lightning-fast boot time and minimal system requirements. Despite its small size, this operating system provides a wide range of built-in applications, including a web browser, text editor, media player, and even some simple games. Its intuitive and user-friendly interface makes it accessible to both novice and experienced users alike. With its emphasis on speed, efficiency, and simplicity, Kolibri OS serves as an excellent choice for those seeking a lightweight operating system without compromising on essential features.
